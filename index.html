Simple Tool to draw end export polyline end-points.

How To use:

  • Start Clicking to draw polyline.
    (There is no need to hold mouse button, Just keep clicking!)
  • Hit "ENTER" or "RETURN" to stop drawing and start a new PolyLine.

Commands:

  • Hold S to activate Snap Mode (Snap mode:active let you to lock the pointer on the exisitng nearest points, like a magnet!)
  • Press U to undo. (no Redo, sorry!)
  • Press C to close the current shape.
  • Press I to show/hide overlayed image (if uploaded any).
  • Press E to export points as a nested Array.
    Like: [ [ [x1,y1], [x2,y2] ], [ [x3,y3], [x4,y4], [x5,y5] ], ...]
